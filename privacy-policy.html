Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information Collection

We collect minimal information necessary for service delivery:

  • Business contact information
  • Service requirements and scope
  • Authorization documentation
  • Communication records

2. Data Protection

All client data is protected using:

  • Encryption for data at rest and in transit
  • Secure communication channels
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments

3. GDPR Compliance

For EU clients, we comply with GDPR requirements:

  • Data minimization principles
  • Right to access and deletion
  • Data processing agreements
  • Breach notification procedures

4. Data Retention

Client data is retained only as necessary for:

  • Service delivery and reporting
  • Legal compliance requirements
  • Continuous service improvement
  • Client-requested documentation

5. Third-Party Disclosure

Client information is never sold or shared except:

  • With explicit client consent
  • As required by law
  • For essential service providers (under NDA)

6. Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security controls:

  • Regular security assessments
  • Employee security training
  • Incident response planning
  • Secure development practices
