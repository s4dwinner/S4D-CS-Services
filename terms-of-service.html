S4D Cyber Security

Acceptable Use Policy

Strictly enforced for all service engagements

1. Authorization Requirement

All security testing and assessment activities require:

  • Written authorization from system owners
  • Clearly defined scope and boundaries
  • Legal right to test all included assets
  • Emergency contact procedures established

2. Permitted Activities

Services are limited to authorized security assessments:

  • Penetration testing with written consent
  • Vulnerability assessments on owned systems
  • Security reviews and architecture analysis
  • Compliance auditing and gap analysis
  • Incident response for client systems

3. Strictly Prohibited Activities

The following activities are never permitted:

  • Unauthorized access to any system
  • Testing without explicit written consent
  • Denial of service attacks or disruptive testing
  • Social engineering without proper authorization
  • Accessing or exfiltrating production data
  • Any activity violating laws or regulations

4. Legal Compliance

All services comply with:

  • Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Applicable state and federal laws
  • Industry ethical standards and guidelines

5. Reporting Requirements

All findings must be:

  • Reported only to authorized contacts
  • Handled with appropriate confidentiality
  • Documented for remediation tracking
  • Shared securely using encrypted channels

6. Violation Consequences

Violation of this policy results in:

  • Immediate termination of services
  • Legal action if applicable
  • Reporting to appropriate authorities
  • Breach of contract penalties
